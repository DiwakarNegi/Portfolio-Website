Brand Personality

Visual references for different interior design styles

Luxury & Sophistication

Opulent materials, rich textures, timeless elegance

Key Characteristics:

  • Gold and bronze metallic accents
  • Rich jewel tones (emerald, sapphire, ruby)
  • Elegant serif typography
  • High-contrast imagery
  • Premium materials showcase

Modern & Minimalist

Clean lines, negative space, functional beauty

Key Characteristics:

  • Monochromatic color schemes
  • Clean sans-serif typography
  • Generous white space
  • Geometric layouts
  • Subtle hover effects

Warm & Inviting

Cozy atmospheres, natural materials, lived-in luxury

Key Characteristics:

  • Warm neutral palettes
  • Organic shapes and curves
  • Natural texture imagery
  • Friendly, approachable fonts
  • Soft shadow effects

Bold & Dramatic

Statement pieces, high contrast, artistic flair

Key Characteristics:

  • High contrast color combinations
  • Bold, display typography
  • Dynamic layouts and angles
  • Strong visual hierarchy
  • Animated transitions
